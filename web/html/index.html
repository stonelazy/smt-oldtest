What We Do ?

A Platform to share your tickets

Our service was designed to provide a common platform for sharing the tickets, any damn tickets that are non-refundable. So, all those guys who won't refund the money on your ticket cancellation, screw em.. you can share your tickets here, find a suitable buyer for your ticket :) Initially we have our website developed only for sharing IRCTC Train tickets but have plans for many more.

What is this site all about ?

The odds are very low that you have a ticket and you can't use it because you got other plans, but when it does you could have gone way past the time you are allowed to cancel your tickets.. Life is often so unpredictable anything might happen at any time and you could be forced to take that journey you weren't planning for and guess what ? Forget Train availability, you can't even find at Redbus.. Stats predict that there is definitely going to be someone else out there who wants to cancel the exact same journey you are dying to get the ticket for. Both of you would have the crisis solved only if you got a common platform to share this info and thats what we do :)
